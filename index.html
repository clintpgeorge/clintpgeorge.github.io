home   About me

I am a postdoc at the Informatics Institute and Department of Statistics, University of Florida with Prof. George Michailidis (Stats). I received Ph.D. and M.S. from the Department of Computer and Information Science and Engineering, University of Florida. Prof. Joseph N. Wilson (CS) and Prof. Hani Doss (Stats) were my advisers. I completed B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from the College of Engineering Thiruvananthapuram.

I work in the following areas of machine learning and applied statistics.

  • Topic models
  • Empirical Bayes methods
  • Markov chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) methods
  • Document clustering and classification
I code @clintpgeorge (Github) and tweet @clintpgeorge (once in a while).
